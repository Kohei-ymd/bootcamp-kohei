Okonomiyaki Recipe

okonomiyaki are savory pancakes from Osaka.

Ingredients

  • 1oog okonomiyaki flour
  • 1ooml water
  • 1egg
  • 1/4cabbage
  • 1spring onion
  • Oil for frying
  • Any meat or seafood of your choice
  • Mayonnaise and Okonomiyaki sauce

Procedure

  1. Whisk together okonomiyaki flour and water.
  2. Chop the cabbage and spring onion.
  3. Mix the egg into the batter.
  4. Thinly slice your protein and set aside.
  5. Heat the oil in a large frying pan or griddle, on medium heat.
  6. Once the pan is hot, add a circle of batter to the pan.
  7. Add the slices of protein on top of the okonomiyaki and cover witha a lid.
  8. After the bottom of the pankake is browned, flip it over
